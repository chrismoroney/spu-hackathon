Theme

Community and Unity

Task: Build a project that incorporates community and unity using any form of technology.

Scope: Projects may be created for any scale, depending on what community the project attempts reach.

In a time where the pandemic has provided difficulties and setbacks in our world, we want to be able to unite with people around us.

Create a program, product, or solution that brings people together in a community during a pandemic.

Rules/Requirements

Requirements: Any submission has to be runnable on our basic computer setup (macOS, Windows, Linux) or have a video submission detailing and showing off your submission

Rules/Legal: 4 team members max

Judging Areas/Criteria

Originality - Is the idea unique? Has it been done before?

Design- Is it designed well? Does it do its job effectively?

Presentation - Is it well presented? Could a development team create/maintain it easily?

Feasibility - How feasible is your project? Can it be accomplished?

Game-Changing Potential - Does this project promote Unity and Community through its function?
