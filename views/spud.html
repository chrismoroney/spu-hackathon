Meet the Team


Chris Moroney

Google DSC Ambassador / SPU Developers Financial Officer

Chris is currently a sophomore at Seattle Pacific University. He is hoping to achieve a Bachelor's of Science (BS) in Computer Science by Spring 2021. Chris's primary responsibilities to SPU Developers is to manage SPU's chapter within the Google DSC Community, provide and educate students with resources provided by Google, host hands-on workshops and labs, and to manage the financial details for the club. Chris joined the SPU Developers Club as a core-team member with an eager desire to learn and teach in the community. Chris wishes to give back to his community by equipping students with skills necessary to starting one's career, as well as to grow all student's passions for programming.

Chris's favorite hobbies includes playing football and basketball outside. On a rainy day, Chris loves to play video games, board games, and watch TV shows. He also is involved with the SPU orchestra and plays both the cello and viola. During the pandemic, Chris has really enjoyed connecting with students online, but really wishes to meet more people in real life.

"A true programmer will always have frustrations on a project. A true programmer also knows how to find solutions through perseverance." - Chris Moroney
